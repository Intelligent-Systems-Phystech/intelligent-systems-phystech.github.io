He graduated with honors from MIPT in 2016. He defended his Ph.D. dissertation on methods of deep learning model selection in 2020, candidate of physical and mathematical sciences. He is an author of more than 29 scientific papers on model selection and natural language processing.

Professional interests: model selection, deep learning, neural networks, Bayesian inference, natural language processing.
