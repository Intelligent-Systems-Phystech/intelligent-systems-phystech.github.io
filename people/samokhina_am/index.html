Alina Samokhina

Personal Website
alina.samokhina@phystech.edu
google scholar
 
 

She graduated from MIPT in 2021. Since 2021 she is a postgraduate student of the Department of Intelligent Systems. She is an author of some scientific papers on machine learning and neuroscience.

Professional interests: brain-computer interfaces, continuous models.
